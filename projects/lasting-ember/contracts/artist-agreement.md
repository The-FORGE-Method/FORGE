## 3. Rights Grant

### 3.1 Exclusive Sync Licensing Rights
Artist hereby grants to Lasting Ember the **exclusive right** to license the submitted Tracks for synchronization ("sync") purposes worldwide. This includes the right to:

- License Tracks for use in film, television, streaming content, web videos, podcasts, video games, advertisements, trailers, and all other audiovisual media
- Sub-license these rights to third-party Licensees
- Create and distribute promotional materials featuring the Tracks
- Issue licenses on Artist's behalf under the terms of Lasting Ember's standard Sync License Agreement

### 3.2 What Artist Retains
Artist **retains**:
- Full ownership of the underlying musical composition and sound recording
- Songwriter credit and publishing rights (subject to Section 5)
- The right to perform the Tracks live
- The right to release the Tracks on streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.) for consumer listening
- The right to sell the Tracks directly to consumers (downloads, physical media)
- Moral rights where applicable

### 3.3 License Duration
This grant is **perpetual** and irrevocable for the term of this Agreement, meaning it continues indefinitely unless terminated under Section 9.

### 3.4 Non-Exclusive Carve-Outs
Artist may grant non-exclusive sync licenses directly **only** with prior written approval from Lasting Ember, to avoid conflicts with existing or pending licenses.

## 6. Performing Rights Organizations (PROs)

### 6.1 PRO Registration Restriction
Artist agrees **NOT** to register the submitted Tracks with any Performing Rights Organization (PRO), including but not limited to:
- ASCAP
- BMI
- SESAC
- GMR
- PRS (UK)
- SOCAN (Canada)
- Or any international equivalent

### 6.2 Reason for Restriction
This restriction exists because:
- Our Licensees expect "cleared" music with no additional fees
- PRO registration creates backend royalty obligations that conflict with our flat-fee licensing model
- Unexpected PRO claims damage Licensee relationships and our platform reputation

### 6.3 Existing PRO Registration
If any Track is already registered with a PRO:
- Artist must disclose this **before** submission
- Artist must withdraw the Track from PRO registration, OR
- The Track may be accepted under modified terms (reduced revenue share to account for PRO complications)

### 6.4 Consequences of Violation
If Lasting Ember or any Licensee incurs costs due to undisclosed PRO claims:
- Artist shall reimburse all costs incurred
- The Track may be removed from the catalog
- This may constitute a material breach under Section 9
