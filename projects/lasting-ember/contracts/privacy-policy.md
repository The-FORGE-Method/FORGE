## 7. Cookies and Tracking

### 7.1 What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit.

### 7.2 Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (Required)
- Authentication and session management
- Security features
- Basic functionality

**Preference Cookies** (Optional)
- Language settings
- Display preferences
- Recent searches

**Analytics Cookies** (Optional)
- Google Analytics or similar
- Usage patterns and trends
- Performance monitoring

**Marketing Cookies** (Optional, with consent)
- Advertising effectiveness
- Retargeting (if applicable)

### 7.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:
- Your browser settings
- Our cookie consent banner (where required)
- Opt-out tools like NAI (networkadvertising.org) or DAA (aboutads.info)

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect Service functionality.

### 7.4 Do Not Track
We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals, as there is no industry standard for this.
