# Lasting Ember

Music licensing platform for original songs — film, TV, games, advertising.

## Timeline
- **Go-live target:** March 1, 2026
- **Current date:** February 7, 2026
- **Days remaining:** 22

## Goals
- 100,000 songs under contract within 6 months
- 1,000,000 songs under contract within 1 year

## Business Model
- **Licensing type:** Buyout (per Jeff's preference)
- Artist submits song → Lasting Ember acquires rights → Licenses to buyers
- One-time payment to artist, clean licensing for buyers

## Agent Architecture (Planned)

### Legal Bot
- Contract generation (artist acquisition agreements)
- Compliance monitoring
- Rights management
- Terms & conditions

### Marketing Bot  
- Artist outreach and acquisition
- Platform promotion
- Content creation
- Social media presence

### Sales Bot
- Outreach to music buyers (film/TV producers, ad agencies, game studios)
- Lead management
- Deal closing
- Account management

### Accounting Bot
- Revenue tracking
- Artist payments
- Financial reporting
- Tax compliance

### Billing Bot
- Invoice generation
- Payment processing
- Subscription management (if applicable)
- Collections

### Catalog Bot
- Song ingestion and metadata
- Categorization and tagging
- Search optimization
- Quality control
