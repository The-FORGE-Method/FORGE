# Radio Station (Division of Lasting Ember)

Internet streaming radio station featuring original music from the Lasting Ember catalog.

## Timeline & Targets
- **Subscriber targets:** 10,000 in 6 months, 100,000 in 1 year
- **Launch:** TBD (after Lasting Ember, or concurrent)

## Structure
Division of Lasting Ember (not separate company) per research recommendation:
- Better PRO negotiating leverage
- Shared compliance infrastructure  
- Data synergies (listening data informs licensing)
- Catalog access deals
- Risk diversification

## Infrastructure Options

### Fast Path: Live365
- All licensing included ($59-299/month)
- Simplest to launch
- Limited customization

### Self-Hosted: AzuraCast
- Free, all-in-one platform
- Built-in SoundExchange reporting
- Full control
- Higher setup effort
- Licensing costs separate (~$2,500+/year minimum)

## Revenue Model
- Subscriptions ($4.99-9.99/month ad-free tier)
- Advertising (CPM $2-25)
- Sponsorships
- Premium features

## Cost Projections
| Scale | Licensing | Infrastructure | Total |
|-------|-----------|----------------|-------|

| 10K subscribers | $8-15K/yr | $2,400/yr | ~$12-18K/yr |
| 100K subscribers | $80-150K/yr | $12K/yr | ~$100-160K/yr |

## Revenue Projections
| Subscribers | @ $5/mo | @ $7.99/mo |
|-------------|---------|------------|
| 10,000 | $50K/mo | $80K/mo |
| 100,000 | $500K/mo | $800K/mo |

## Required Licensing
- ASCAP
- BMI  
- SESAC
- GMR
- SoundExchange (if playing non-catalog content)

*Note: If only playing Lasting Ember catalog (artist-assigned sync rights, no PRO registration), licensing burden is significantly reduced.*
